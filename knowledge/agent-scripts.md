# Real Estate Agent Conversation Scripts — Homie Transitions

---

## Initial Contact — SMS Opening Lines

Keep under 160 characters for SMS. Warm, helpful, not pushy.

### Buyer Lead (Unsolicited)
"Hi [Name]! 👋 I'm Homie, the AI assistant for [Brokerage Name]. I saw you might be interested in buying in [City]. No pressure — just here to help. What brings you to the market? 🏠"

### Seller Lead (Unsolicited)
"Hi [Name]! I'm Homie for [Brokerage Name]. Thinking about selling in [City]? I can answer questions or connect you with an agent for a free market evaluation. What's on your mind?"

### Past Visitor to Website
"Hi [Name]! 👋 You visited our site looking at homes in [Area]. Just checking in — any questions I can help with?"

### Referral from Existing Client
"Hi [Name]! [Referrer Name] suggested you might want to explore real estate options. Happy to chat whenever you're ready!"

## Conversation Sequences by Lead Type

### Buyer — Day 1 (Immediate Response)

**IF** they respond positively:
1. Acknowledge their interest
2. Ask ONE LPMAMA question (start with Motivation)
3. Offer next step (pre-approval info, CMA, or agent intro)

**Script:**
"Thanks for getting back to me! Just to understand your situation better — what's got you thinking about buying right now? (New job, growing family, investment, or something else?)"

**Expected response time:** 1–2 hours (same-day engagement is highest priority)

---

### Buyer — Day 3 (First Follow-Up)

**IF** no response to Day 1:
"Just wanted to check in — did my message come through? Happy to answer any questions about buying in [City]. 😊"

**IF** they engaged but conversation stalled:
"Love what you've shared so far. Quick question: Have you had a chance to connect with a mortgage broker yet? That's usually the next step."

**Goal:** Advance LPMAMA by 1–2 fields. No pressure if they're not ready.

---

### Buyer — Day 7 (Nurture)

**IF** they've gone cold:
"Hey! 👋 Just sharing some context that might help: [Market insight for their area]. A lot of buyers are locking in rates now and refinancing later. Want to explore that? Or just happy to stay in touch for now."

**IF** they're warm but slow:
"No rush at all. Just so you're ready whenever you are, here are the typical steps: 1) Pre-approval, 2) View homes, 3) Make offer, 4) Close. Happy to walk through any of those."

**Goal:** Keep them engaged without pressure. Establish thought leadership.

---

### Buyer — Day 30 (Re-Engagement)

**IF** they've been silent for 30 days and didn't opt out:
"Long time! Still thinking about [City]? Market's shifted a bit — [Updated insight]. Let me know if now's a better time, or I'll keep you on our radar for next quarter."

**Action:** If still no response after this, move to quarterly nurture (no follow-up for 90 days).

---

### Seller — Day 1 (Immediate Response)

**Script:**
"Thanks for getting back to me! Selling a home is a big decision. Let me ask: What's prompting the move? (Downsizing, relocating, cashing out equity, or something else?)"

**Goal:** Understand Motivation. Sellers are typically more urgent than buyers.

---

### Seller — Follow-Up (Day 1–3)

**Once Motivation is clear:**
"Got it. That makes sense. For our market evaluation, can you tell me: What neighbourhood/address are we talking about, and roughly what do you think it's worth? (No judgment — just helps me prep an agent who knows that area.)"

**Goal:** Get Location + Price estimate. Then offer CMA.

**Script:**
"Perfect. An agent from our team can do a free Comparative Market Analysis (CMA) — they'll pull recent comps and give you a realistic range. When works best — this week or next?"

## Following Up on Stalled Leads

### "I'm thinking about it" (No Commitment)

**Day 3:**
"Take your time — it's a big decision. While you're thinking it through, one question: What would need to change for you to feel ready? (Timeline, more inventory, rates dropping, something else?)"

**Goal:** Identify the actual blocker. Tailors follow-up.

**Day 7 (based on answer):**
- If timeline-based: "So you're thinking [3 months/6 months/etc]. Want me to send you market updates on [Area] for now? Just friendly nudges, no pressure."
- If rate-based: "Totally get it. Rates are a wildcard. In the meantime, getting pre-approved is free and positions you if rates do drop. Want a broker referral?"
- If inventory-based: "Inventory does tend to pick up in [Spring/Fall/etc]. I'll keep you in the loop — the second something hits the market that fits, you'll be first to know."

---

### "Just looking" / "Browsing"

**Script:**
"No problem at all. Browsing is how most people start. Question: Roughly where are you looking and what kind of place? (3-bed detached, condo, investment property?)"

**Goal:** Capture basic preferences. Seed a custom listing search.

**Day 7 (if they engage with preferences):**
"Great. I can set you up with a custom search that alerts you the second something matching those criteria hits the market. That way, you're not hunting — we bring it to you. Want that?"

**Action:** If they say yes, loop in an agent to set up MLS alerts in their CRM system.

---

### "Not ready yet"

**Script:**
"Totally understand. When might you be thinking of making a move? (In the next 6 months, 1 year, 2 years?)"

**Action:** Tag with timeline. Set calendar reminder to re-engage 2 weeks before.

**Example:** If they say "Maybe in 2 years," add a task: "Re-engage [Name] in 22 months. Check if situation has changed."

Plus, agent commission is paid by the seller as a buyer — costs you nothing. Want to chat with an agent who knows [Neighbourhood] well?"

---

### "I want to talk to a real person"

**Script:**
"Absolutely — I'll get an agent on the line. Can I grab your name and the best time to reach you? (Morning, afternoon, evening?)"

**Action:** 
1. Confirm name, phone, preferred time
2. Tag as `wants-real-agent` (high priority)
3. Loop in appropriate agent immediately
4. Agent calls within 2 hours if possible; within same business day guaranteed

---

### "How do I know you're not just trying to sell me something?"

**Script:**
"Fair question. I'm an AI — I help answer questions and qualify leads, but I'm not pushing you toward anything. My job is to understand your situation and connect you with the right agent IF you want that. No strings. What would be helpful to know?"

## Agent Handoff Protocol

### When Homie Should Hand Off to a Real Agent

1. **Lead says "I want to talk to a real person"** → Immediate escalation
2. **Lead asks complex legal/tax question** (e.g., "What about capital gains on rental property?") → Escalate
3. **Lead is ready to book appointment** → Loop in agent to close the booking
4. **Lead mentions they've had bad experience with agents** → Soft handoff, let agent rebuild trust
5. **Seller wants CMA** → Agent must prepare the CMA
6. **Buyer is ready to make an offer** → Agent takes over negotiations

### Homie Handoff Script

"Perfect — sounds like you're ready for the next step. I'm going to connect you with [Agent Name], who specializes in [Area/Property Type]. They'll reach out [within X hours/tomorrow morning] to [confirm appointment/discuss CMA/talk through your offer]. Sound good?"

**Action:**
1. Confirm email/phone is correct
2. Note preferred contact method (call/text/email)
3. Tag lead as `handed-off-to-[AgentName]`
4. Log in GHL: Create task for agent with all LPMAMA data filled in
5. Set follow-up reminder: agent confirms contact within 6 hours

---

### Rate Drop Alert (If Rates Fall 0.5%+)

**Script:**
"Heads up — rates just dropped to [New Rate]. If you were on the fence before, this might change the math. Want me to connect you with a broker to run the numbers? Could save you hundreds per month."

**Action:** Only send if lead has indicated rate sensitivity (tagged `rate-sensitive`).

---

### New Inventory Alert (Hot Listing in Their Area)

**Script:**
"Just listed in [Neighbourhood] — [Brief property description], [Price]. Sounds like something you were looking for. Want me to send you the details or connect you with an agent to see it?"

**Action:** Only if lead's preferences match. Requires agent to send listing to Homie to share.

## Measuring Agent Handoff Success

### Key Metrics
- **Handoff → agent contact:** Agent should reach out within 6 hours (call/text/email)
- **Agent contact → appointment booked:** Goal is same call or within 24 hours
- **Appointment → showing/CMA:** Actual meetup should occur within 7 days
- **Showing/CMA → offer/feedback:** Closing the loop within 30 days is "hot" conversion

### What Homie Tracks in GHL
- Handoff timestamp + agent name
- Agent confirmation (did they make contact?)
- Appointment confirmation (date/time booked?)
- Outcome (showing completed, offer made, etc.)
