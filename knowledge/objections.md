# Lead Objections — Homie Handling Scripts

## "Not interested"

**Script:**
"Totally respect that — no pressure at all. Would it be okay if I kept your info on file in case your situation changes down the road? I won't bother you, just want to make sure you have a point of contact when the time is right."

**Action:** Tag as `cold-lead`, set a 90-day re-engagement task. Do not follow up sooner unless the lead re-engages.

---

## "Too expensive" / "I can't afford it"

**Script:**
"I hear you — the market has been tough. A few things that might help: the FHSA lets first-time buyers save up to $40K tax-free, and with CMHC insurance you can get in with as little as 5% down. There are also more affordable options in some neighbourhoods that often get overlooked. Would it be worth a quick chat to explore what's actually within reach for you?"

**Action:** Offer price range discussion, refer to mortgage broker if needed. Ask LPMAMA budget question.

---

## "Bad timing" / "Not the right time"

**Script:**
"That's completely fair — timing matters. Can I ask what would need to change for the timing to feel right? Sometimes we can plan around a timeline, and our agents are great at helping figure out the best window. In the meantime, would you like me to keep you updated on the market so you're ready when the time comes?"

**Action:** Add to nurture sequence. Set a follow-up reminder for the timeline they mention (e.g., 3 months, 6 months).

---

## "I need to sell my home first"

**Script:**
"That's very common — most people need the equity from their current home to fund the next one. Our agents are experienced with back-to-back transactions and can help you coordinate both sides so you're not left in a gap. Want me to set up a call to talk through the options?"

**Action:** Flag as dual transaction opportunity. Loop in an agent who handles both buying and selling.

---

## "I'm going with another agent" / "I already have an agent"

**Script:**
"Completely understand — having the right agent is everything. I hope it goes smoothly for you! If you ever need a second opinion or things don't work out, please don't hesitate to reach out. We're always here."

**Action:** Mark as `has-agent`. Do not follow up unless they re-engage. Leave door open gracefully.

---

## "Rates are too high" / "Waiting for rates to drop"

**Script:**
"You're not alone in thinking that — rates have definitely shifted the math for a lot of buyers. That said, when rates drop, demand usually spikes and prices follow. Some buyers are locking in now and planning to refinance later. Would it be worth a conversation with one of our agents or a mortgage broker to look at the numbers for your specific situation?"

**Action:** Share rate context. Offer mortgage broker referral. Add to nurture list for rate drop alerts.

---

## "Stop texting me" / "Remove me" / "Unsubscribe"

**Script:**
"Absolutely — I'm removing you from all communications right now. I'm sorry for any inconvenience. You won't hear from us again unless you reach out. Take care!"

**Action:** IMMEDIATE opt-out. Apply `dnc` tag in GHL. Stop all automated follow-ups. Log opt-out with timestamp. This is non-negotiable — compliance is mandatory.

---

## "I don't trust AI" / "I don't want to talk to a bot"

**Script:**
"Completely fair — I'll get a real agent on the line for you. Can I get your name and the best time to reach you? They'll call personally."

**Action:** Escalate to agent immediately. Do not continue automated conversation.

---

## "I'm just looking at my options"

**Script:**
"That's a great place to start. Exploring your options is the first step. Is there a particular area or type of property you've been thinking about? Even a broad idea helps us point you in the right direction."

**Action:** Nurture gently. Ask one qualifying question. Don't push for appointment.

---

## "Your prices are too high" / "Royal LePage charges too much"

**Script:**
"I understand the concern — commission is a real cost. What I can tell you is that our agents work hard to maximize your sale price, which often more than offsets the fee. And as a buyer, you typically pay nothing for agent representation. Would it help to talk through what the numbers actually look like for your situation?"

**Action:** Educate on value, not just cost. Offer to connect with an agent for a transparent conversation.
